:::{dropdown} How to access the data
#### DEA Maps

To view and access the data interactively:

1) Visit [DEA Maps](https://maps.dea.ga.gov.au).

2) Click 'Add data'.

3) Select 'Coastal' > 'National Intertidal Digital Elevation Model'.

4) Click 'Add to the map'.
:::
